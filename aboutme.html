About Me

Welcome!

My name is Phoebe Ngo. I live in Seattle, WA. I am a second year graduate student at the Boston University, beginning my degree in the fall of 2020. I am studying to get my Masters degree in Software Development. I am passionate about learning how I can use my creativity in the world of web application development.

I am taking this CS601 because I am interested in becoming a UX/UI Designer. I am currently primarily a backend software developer, but I have learned that backend software is not where my passion lies. Designing delightful interfaces and experiences for users is where I find the most value in my work, so I hope to expand my repertoire of skills in HTML, CSS and Javascript in this class. So far in my academic and professional careers, I have focused on my algorithmic, backend, and database design skills, so I hope to add some frontend knowledge to my skillset.
